# Content-to-Lead Services

## Purpose

Operate a repeatable content and lead-generation service for websites, digital agencies, internal ventures, and approved client work.

The service does not sell isolated blog posts or generic AI writing. It delivers a connected system:

```text
Search question
  → useful page or article
  → lead magnet
  → email capture
  → delivery and nurture
  → consultation, quote, or sale
  → analytics and refresh
```

## Service Standard

Every campaign must connect content to a measurable business outcome.

Each major topic should eventually include:

1. One service or pillar page
2. Three to six supporting articles
3. One downloadable guide, checklist, workbook, template, scorecard, or calculator
4. One landing page
5. One email capture form
6. One delivery email
7. Two or three nurture emails
8. Conversion and search tracking
9. A scheduled performance review

## Core Capabilities

- SEO research and topic clusters
- Search-intent content briefs
- Client expertise capture
- Human-reviewed AI-assisted writing
- AEO and GEO content structures
- Lead-magnet copy and design
- Landing pages and forms
- Delivery and nurture sequences
- CMS publishing
- Analytics and reporting
- Content refresh and consolidation
- Google Maps/Places local account discovery
- Apollo account and contact discovery
- Clay enrichment orchestration and scoring
- LinkedIn research and relationship building
- CRM handoff and attribution
- Client approval, compliance, and evidence controls

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- Do not invent facts, prices, credentials, testimonials, case studies, statistics, contacts, consent, or results.
- Do not publish regulated advice without qualified review.
- Do not create pages that compete with an existing canonical page.
- Do not hide all useful information behind an email form.
- Do not optimize for word count or keyword density.
- Do not request passwords through ordinary email.
- Do not scrape Google Maps or LinkedIn or bypass access controls.
- Do not install overlapping tools without a documented recurring need and ROI.
- Do not automate factual approval, final editorial judgment, qualification, sensitive claims, outreach, or compliance review in the initial system.
- Every published asset and prospect record needs a purpose, source, owner, next action, evidence status, and review date.

## Success Measures

- Qualified leads generated
- Landing-page conversion rate
- Organic impressions and clicks
- Non-branded search visibility
- Service-page conversions
- Email delivery, open, and click performance
- Verified-account and contact rate
- Positive reply and meeting rate
- Time from brief to publication
- Revision rounds
- On-time publication rate
- Cost per article, guide, qualified lead, and opportunity
- Content refresh impact
- Client approval time
- Revenue attributed to content or lead source where evidence exists
