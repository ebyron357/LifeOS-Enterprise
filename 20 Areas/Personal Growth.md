# Personal Growth

## Purpose

Become stronger, healthier, wiser, calmer, and more effective through small actions that can be repeated.

## Growth Areas

| Area | What good progress means |
|---|---|
| Purpose | My time matches what matters most. |
| Health | I support sleep, movement, food, and medical care. |
| Mind | I protect focus, peace, and emotional safety. |
| Learning | I learn useful ideas and apply them. |
| Relationships | I give attention to the people who matter. |
| Money | I make informed choices and reduce avoidable stress. |
| Work | I move the highest-value work forward. |
| Service | I use my growth to help other people. |

## Weekly Standard

- Complete one honest weekly check-in.
- Record wins with evidence.
- Name the main point of friction.
- Choose one small next action.
- Ask for support when support would help.
- Do not treat a difficult week as failure.

## Current Focus

Build consistency without overload. The system should reduce mental load, not create more work.
