## Operating Rules

- Every course must support an active project, certification, revenue path, or specific capability.
- Limit active learning tracks to three at one time.
- Each session must produce evidence: notes, a completed lab, a certification milestone, code, a workflow, or a client-ready asset.
- Do not count passive video watching as completion.
- Review subscriptions and paid plans monthly.
- Move finished resources into the appropriate project, SOP, or reference note.
