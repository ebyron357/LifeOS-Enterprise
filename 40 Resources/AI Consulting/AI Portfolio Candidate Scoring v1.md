# AI Portfolio Candidate Scoring v1

## Purpose

Select the first three AI consulting portfolio flagships using current evidence rather than visual appeal, personal attachment, or unsupported assumptions.

This is a preliminary evidence review. Repository ownership under a GitHub account does not by itself prove legal ownership, client publication rights, trademark rights, or permission to disclose business information.

## Scoring Model

| Factor | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Evidence strength | 25 |
| Relevance to target buyer | 20 |
| Demonstrates a paid consulting capability | 20 |
| Working or production readiness | 15 |
| Permission and privacy readiness | 10 |
| Visual and storytelling strength | 5 |
| Ability to produce public content | 5 |
| **Total** | **100** |

## Executive Decision

### Selected Flagships

1. **LifeOS Enterprise** — selected now.
2. **TradeIQ Command Center** — selected now as a working AI product and evaluation case, explicitly labeled as pre-production.
3. **AI Readiness and Adoption Toolkit** — selected as the direct consulting proof asset to build next; not publishable until the toolkit and demonstration output exist.

### Reserve Candidates

- **ClientVerse** — reserve; strong production-modernization evidence, but its current public release does not expose an AI assistant or paid AI endpoint. Use only after the case-study angle is narrowed to verified modernization, workflow, governance, or a later AI implementation.
- **Shopify AI Store and App Systems** — reserve and permission-gated; code evidence exists for Storefront API products and cart creation, but the candidate mixes internal, demonstration, and possible client contexts.
- **Charlotte Real Estate AI Platform** — reserve and client-permission blocked; technical evidence is strong, but public use requires written approval or a completely sanitized demonstration.
- **AI Education Initiative** — future mission case; program and launch evidence are currently insufficient.
- **Standalone AI Agent Demonstration** — merge into TradeIQ initially or build as a separate controlled demonstration later; avoid duplicating proof.

## Portfolio Sequence

### Flagship 1 — LifeOS Enterprise

**Why first:** Most directly aligned with the consulting position, public by design, and controlled internally.

**Next action:** Complete a verified evidence package and walkthrough.

### Flagship 2 — TradeIQ

**Why second:** Strongest current technical AI implementation, human-control, fallback, security, and evaluation evidence.

**Next action:** Run validation, verify preview, capture demo, and document production limitations.

### Flagship 3 — AI Readiness and Adoption Toolkit

**Why third:** Converts technical credibility into a buyer-ready consulting offer.

**Next action:** Build the toolkit and a complete fictional or approved sample engagement.
