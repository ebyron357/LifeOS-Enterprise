# Operationalize Content-to-Lead Service

## Desired Outcome

Create a repeatable, measurable agency service that moves one commercial topic through:

```text
Research
  → content brief
  → expert input
  → article or service page
  → lead magnet
  → landing page and form
  → delivery and nurture
  → CRM handoff
  → lead follow-up
  → analytics and refresh
```

## Definition of Done

- [ ] One service positioning statement is approved.
- [ ] Four service packages are documented.
- [ ] One target niche and one ideal client profile are selected for the pilot.
- [ ] Client intake, access, baseline, content brief, content QA, publishing, lead-magnet, and reporting templates exist.
- [ ] The content production Base renders with all required stages.
- [ ] The lead prospecting Base renders with prospect, enrichment, outreach, and handoff stages.
- [ ] Clay, Apollo, Google Maps/Places, LinkedIn, and manual research roles are documented with cost and compliance gates.
- [ ] One content cluster is complete: pillar/service page, three supporting articles, one lead magnet, one landing page, delivery email, and nurture sequence.
- [ ] One pilot lead list is created and verified without inventing contact information.
- [ ] Outreach messages are based on a real observed content or website gap.
- [ ] CRM or approved tracker receives qualified prospects and lead source data.
- [ ] GA4 and Search Console measurement plan is documented.
- [ ] One monthly reporting template is complete.
- [ ] One campaign runs from research to measurable conversion without rebuilding the process.
- [ ] Privacy, consent, opt-out, and regulated-content review rules are documented.
- [ ] Tool subscriptions are limited to one primary tool per major job unless ROI justifies overlap.

## 14-Day Implementation Plan

### Days 1–2 — Offer and Pipeline

- Choose the pilot niche and offer.
- Define ideal client profile and exclusion rules.
- Create content and prospecting pipelines.
- Define statuses, fields, ownership, and review rules.

### Days 3–4 — Onboarding and Baseline

- Create the client intake form.
- Create access and permissions checklist.
- Create Drive or connected storage structure.
- Create baseline report and approved-claims register.

### Days 5–6 — Content System

- Create content brief and expertise interview templates.
- Create SEO/AEO/GEO editorial QA.
- Create publishing and refresh checklists.
- Create one controlled client/project AI workspace specification.

### Days 7–8 — Lead Magnet and Funnel

- Create lead-magnet brief and reusable design specification.
- Create landing-page, thank-you-page, form, delivery-email, and nurture templates.
- Define form and CRM data contract.

### Days 9–10 — Lead Acquisition

- Define Google Maps/Places local-account discovery workflow.
- Define Apollo contact discovery and enrichment workflow.
- Define Clay waterfall enrichment and scoring workflow.
- Define LinkedIn research and relationship workflow.
- Define verification, suppression, opt-out, and duplicate controls.

### Days 11–12 — Analytics and Automation

- Define content, search, lead, and revenue metrics.
- Create event and attribution plan.
- Automate only stable handoffs: status notifications, task creation, approved CRM sync, and review reminders.

### Days 13–14 — Pilot

- Run one commercial topic through the full content system.
- Build one verified prospect list for the selected niche.
- Send a small reviewed outreach pilot.
- Test the lead magnet, form, delivery, nurture, CRM handoff, and analytics.
- Record problems, remove unnecessary steps, and update SOPs.
